# Assembly process of the [Scrabblepad](https://geekhack.org/index.php?topic=96321.0)
## This repo documents the assembly process of the Scrabblepad, a 15x15 keyboard/macropad/monstrosity designed and sold by Donut Cat.
![Switch city](IMG_20181204_214143.jpg?raw=true)

## 1. Bend and insert all diodes. Make sure the black bar on the diode is at the side of the thicker white bar on the PCB.
![Insert diodes](IMG_20181204_180400.jpg?raw=true)

## 2. Bend legs of diodes so they don't fall out.
![Bend diodes](IMG_20181204_201354.jpg?raw=true)

## 3. Solder diodes and cut off legs.
![Diodes with cut off legs](IMG_20181204_211620.jpg?raw=true)

## 4. Insert the Teensy++ header. Tip: put Teensy++ over headers to properly align the headers. Solder the headers. DO NOT solder the Teensy++ yet.
![Teensy++ header](IMG_20181204_211645.jpg?raw=true)

## 5. Insert all your switches.
![switches](IMG_20181204_214124.jpg?raw=true)

## 6. Solder all your switches.
![Soldered switches](IMG_20181205_150554.jpg?raw=true)

## 7. Insert your Teensy++ and solder it with the components facing the Scrabblepad.
![Teensy++ installed](IMG_20181205_151050.jpg?raw=true)
